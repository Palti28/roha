### 01 — What it is

A website template for construction companies, civil contractors, and infrastructure firms. Heavy, confident, and direct — the design communicates scale and capability without overselling.

The palette is dark and amber-accented. Typography is sharp and geometric. The structure is built around a client who is evaluating whether to hand you a significant project: they want proof of volume, evidence of quality, and a clear path to contact. Every section earns its place in that argument.

Stats lead the hero. Services are laid out as a clear menu. A dark quality section signals conviction. Projects are shown large. Testimonials close the case.

### 02 — What's inside

- Full-page HTML template: hero with animated counters, services, about, partners marquee, quality statement, projects, testimonials, CTA, footer
- Hero with live counter stats (projects completed, years of experience, client satisfaction)
- Services grid with four offering cards and hover states
- About section with expandable accordion for detailed firm background
- Partners marquee strip for trust signals
- Dark-background quality section with bold statement copy
- Projects showcase with two featured cards and category labels
- Three-column testimonial grid with client attribution
- CTA section with email capture form
- Glassmorphism navigation with scroll-state transition
- Scroll-triggered reveal animations throughout
- Fully responsive, mobile-first (320–1440)
- Self-contained: all CSS and JS inlined, zero dependencies

### 03 — Spec & details

- **Format**: HTML / CSS / JS
- **Software**: Any code editor
- **Font**: Space Grotesk, Inter (Google Fonts)
- **License**: Personal + Commercial

### 04 — Who it's for

Construction companies, civil engineering firms, and large-scale contractors that need a site capable of handling enterprise client scrutiny. Works equally for a regional contractor building credibility and an established firm refreshing its digital presence before a major tender.
