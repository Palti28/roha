§ 01 — About the practice

Architecture and craft held together, on one team.

An integrated design-and-build studio for considered residences.

Northwood was founded by two siblings — an architect and a contractor — who were tired of watching beautiful drawings die on muddy sites. We bring the design table and the build site into the same room, the same Slack, and the same accountability.

Our clients are owners who care more about how a wall meets a floor than how a foyer photographs. We work slowly, we cost honestly, and we deliver homes that get more comfortable over time.

Adi & Renata Northwood
Founding Partners

Licensed, insured, accountable.

The boring documents that make the work possible.
