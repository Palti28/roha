### 01 — What it is

A website template for professional service firms where authority and restraint matter more than personality. Editorial layout, precise typography, and a tone that signals seriousness without stiffness.

The layout is deliberately dense and text-forward: long-form sections, numbered practice areas, team bios with full credentials, a publications archive. This is a firm that earns trust through substance, and the site reflects that.

Color is monochrome with a single warm accent. The typeface is editorial serif for headlines and a clean sans for body copy, chosen to feel like a well-designed legal brief rather than a marketing deck.

### 02 — What's inside

- Full-page HTML template: hero statement, practice areas, team roster, client sectors, publications, contact
- Practice areas section with numbered index and expandable descriptions
- Team grid with photo, title, education, and bar admission details
- Client sector icons showing the industries served
- Publications and insights section with date-sorted card layout
- Formal contact section with office address and direct lines
- Scroll-triggered fade animations applied with restraint
- Fully responsive, mobile-first (320–1440)
- Self-contained: all CSS and JS inlined, zero dependencies

### 04 — Who it's for

Law firms, management consultancies, financial advisory practices, and any professional services business whose clients expect substance over style. Also works for a senior individual practitioner who needs a personal firm site.
