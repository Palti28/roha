04 · Logo Usage

Keep it
clean.

DO
Use on cream, forest green, or near-black backgrounds only
Maintain clear space equal to the height of the leaf mark on all sides
Use the reversed (cream) version on dark backgrounds
Minimum size: 24px height for digital, 10mm for print
Keep wordmark and mark together — don't separate without approval
DON’T
Don't stretch, rotate, or distort the mark
Don't change the leaf colour — it must remain Leaf (#8CC968) or Cream (#F4EFE3)
Don't place on busy photographic backgrounds without a solid overlay
Don't use any colour not in the brand palette for the logo
Don't add shadows, glows, or decorative effects to the logo
