Most business websites don't fail because of bad design. They fail because the fundamentals weren't checked before launch: unclear messaging, broken forms, missing meta tags, no real photos.

This checklist covers the things that matter most. Not a design framework, not a 50-point audit. Just the decisions and checks that separate a website that builds trust from one that quietly loses it.

Go through it before you hit publish.
