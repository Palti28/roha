Frequently asked

The things our patients usually ask.

Don't see your question? Send us a WhatsApp; one of our nurses replies within the hour during clinic hours.

01Do I really need a consultation before booking treatment?+
Yes. Even for routine treatments, a 20-minute consultation lets the practising doctor assess your skin type, medical history, and goals, and prescribe something genuinely suitable. Consultations are complimentary if you book a treatment within 30 days.
02Are your injectables and lasers FDA / BPOM registered?+
Every product we use is BPOM-registered, and the majority are also FDA / CE marked. We can show you the batch number, expiry, and certificate before any treatment begins.
03How much does a typical treatment plan cost?+
Most plans fall between IDR 4M and 15M total over 3–6 months. We build a written, fixed plan after consultation and never upsell during the treatment session itself.
04Can I do a treatment on my lunch break?+
Many of our facials and skin boosters are designed for short windows. Injectables have a 24-hour avoid-makeup recommendation. We'll always tell you the realistic downtime up front.
05Do you treat first-time aesthetic patients?+
Often — and we love working with them. The doctor will spend the first session explaining what's actually happening to your skin, before recommending anything more involved than a facial.
06Is parking available at the clinic?+
Yes — the building offers complimentary 3-hour parking for clinic patients. We'll send you the location pin and parking instructions when you confirm your appointment.
