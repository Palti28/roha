How We Work

Slow at the start, quick at the end.

01

Briefing

1–2 days

Discovery call, scope alignment. We make sure we understand the problem first.

02

Exploration

3–5 days

Mood board, wireframes, direction. Two or three directions to test before committing.

03

Design

1–3 weeks

Visual design, prototype, interaction details. Reviews every week.

04

Iteration

1 week

One big revision round, not small ping-pong loops that wear everyone out.

05

Handoff

1–2 days

File delivery, documentation, and 2 weeks of handoff support.
