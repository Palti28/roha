### 01 — What it is

A website template for build, architecture, and interior firms. Clean layouts and generous white space let project photography carry the weight, with copy structured to qualify clients before the first call.

The design leans warm: earthy tones, heavy serif accents, and a restrained palette that feels materials-aware without being rustic. It reads credible to a client with a serious budget and accessible to someone renovating their first home.

Structure follows the client journey: understand the firm, browse services, see past work, understand the process, make contact.

### 02 — What's inside

- Full-page HTML template: hero, services, project gallery, process steps, testimonials, contact
- Project portfolio grid with hover overlay and category filtering
- Four-step process section with numbered layout
- Services breakdown with description panels
- Testimonial strip with client attribution
- Contact section with location detail
- Scroll-triggered fade animations throughout
- Fully responsive, mobile-first (320–1440)
- Self-contained: all CSS and JS inlined, zero dependencies

### 04 — Who it's for

Contractors, interior designers, and small architecture firms that need a site that earns trust from a client who is about to spend serious money on their space. Replace the placeholder project images with your own work and it's ready.
